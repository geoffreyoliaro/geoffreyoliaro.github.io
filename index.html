About Me

A self taught programmer, in my free time I play around with Javascript and Python.

I am an avid reader and a tinkerer of anything that can manipulate data and its security thereof. In my many exploits i have come to find that python as a programming language is well suited both for data analytics and computer security. For data analytics it has powerfull frameworks and libraries such as numpy and pandas. On the other hand python as tool for security has enabled the development of tools such as burpsuite that is useful in web penetration testing

Javascript in skilled hands is nothing less but a swiss army knife on the go. Its ability to automate the most mundane of tasks makes it very useful when it comes to web development and even desktop application development. Some of its well known frameworks such as jquery, vue.js and react.js allow programmers to be able to liven up activity on the client side of a website.
